Welcome to the Continuous COVID-19 Monitoring app!

With the help of our system, doctors and nurses can monitor the COVID-19 positive patients from far and safe distances on their screen monitors. Our system, with the help of the Internet of Things, will dynamically flash the list of patients in decreasing order of their severity by continuously monitoring via sensors. This way, a single medical staff can look after more patients than they generally can in a time of crisis. Also, there is reduced contact and threat for frontline workers with the help of our system.
